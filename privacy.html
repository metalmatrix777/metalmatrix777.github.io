Privacy Policy — Fact Vault Stats

Last updated: July 7, 2026

1. Overview

Fact Vault Stats ("the App") is a personal-use tool that connects to the TikTok account of its operator ("the Operator") to read profile and video statistics and to publish videos to the Operator's own channel. This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is handled.

2. Data Accessed

With the Operator's authorization via TikTok Login Kit, the App may access: basic profile information (display name, username, avatar); account statistics (follower count, following count, likes count, video count); and video metadata and statistics (titles, view counts, likes, comments, shares). The App does not access data belonging to any TikTok user other than the Operator.

3. How Data Is Used

Data is used solely to generate personal analytics reports for the Operator and to publish the Operator's own content. Data is not sold, shared, rented, or disclosed to any third party, and is not used for advertising or profiling.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Access tokens are stored securely by the authentication provider and are used only to make authorized API requests. Statistics may be retained in personal report files controlled by the Operator. No user database is maintained.

5. Data Deletion and Revocation

Authorization can be revoked at any time from the TikTok account's "Manage app permissions" security settings, which immediately ends the App's access. Stored reports can be deleted by the Operator at any time. Deletion requests can be sent to the contact address below.

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses TikTok's official APIs, subject to TikTok's own Privacy Policy and Developer Terms.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time; the latest version applies.
